# ET Money Mentor

ET Money Mentor is a comprehensive platform aimed at financial education and investment guidance. Our goal is to empower users with the knowledge and skills necessary to manage their finances effectively and make informed investment decisions.

## Features
- **Financial Education**: Access to a wealth of resources and articles on personal finance, budgeting, and financial planning.
- **Investment Guidance**: Expert insights and strategies for various types of investments, including stocks, bonds, and mutual funds.
- **User Community**: Engage with other learners and experts in our community forums to share experiences and advice.
- **Interactive Tools**: Utilize our financial calculators and tools to assist with budgeting, investment planning, and more.

## Getting Started
To start using ET Money Mentor:
1. Create an account on our website.
2. Explore the resources available in the Education section.
3. Join discussions in the community forums.
4. Use our tools to assist in your financial journey.

## Technologies Used
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Backend: Node.js, Express
- Database: MongoDB
